Machine Mastery Matrix

Comprehensive technical specifications for global embroidery systems. Vusi’s diagnostic expertise covers the full spectrum of industrial and commercial platforms.

1. Tajima (Japan)

Tajima is the premium industrial standard for high-speed manufacturing.

  • TMEZ-SC Series: 1 Head (Single-head flagship with Artificial Intelligence thread management).
  • TMEZ-KC Series: 2 Heads, 4 Heads, 6 Heads, or 8 Heads (Multi-head AI configurations).
  • TMAR-V Series: 2 Heads, 4 Heads, 6 Heads, or 8 Heads (Heavy-duty industrial arm type).
  • TFMX-C Series: 2 Heads, 4 Heads, 6 Heads, or 8 Heads (Standard commercial workhorse).
  • TMX-TL Series: 1 Head (Specialized single-head machine built for extra-large embroidery fields).
  • TCMX Series: 4 Heads, 6 Heads, 12 Heads, or up to 30 Heads (Specialty mix-type chenille and lockstitch machines).

2. Ricoma (USA / China)

Ricoma covers a massive range from boutique setups to corporate production lines.

  • EM-1010 Series: 1 Head (10-needle entry-level home studio model).
  • TC Series (RCM-1201TC / 1501TC): 1 Head (12 or 15-needle commercial compact model).
  • MT Series (MT-1501 / MT-1502): 1 Head or 2 Heads (High-clearance open chassis models).
  • SWD Series: 1 Head (Super wide-field single head for heavy banners or blankets).
  • CHT2 Series: 2 Heads, 4 Heads, 6 Heads, 8 Heads, or 12 Heads (Industrial multi-head platform).
  • RCM-E Series (e.g., RCM-0604E): 4 Heads or 6 Heads (Traditional standard multi-head).
  • WCH Series: 12 Heads, 15 Heads, or 20 Heads (Ultra-wide multi-head for continuous heavy roll fabrics).

3. Barudan (Japan)

Barudan is favored globally for unparalleled structural durability on heavy fabrics like leather or thick canvas.

  • BEKT Series (Pro 3): 1 Head (High-speed single head commercial unit).
  • BEKY Series: 2 Heads, 4 Heads, 6 Heads, or 8 Heads (High-efficiency multi-head bridge line).
  • BEKS Series: 2 Heads, 4 Heads, 6 Heads, 8 Heads, 12 Heads, or 15 Heads (Heavy industrial chassis).
  • BEXS / BEXY Series: 4 Heads, 6 Heads, 12 Heads, 20 Heads, or up to 30 Heads (Massive industrial flat-bed scaling models).
  • BEKS-CL / CLZ Series: 4 Heads, 6 Heads, 12 Heads, or 20 Heads (Specialized multi-head chenille and chain-stitch variations).

4. Brother (Japan)

Brother dominates the craft, small business storefront, and home workshop spaces.

  • Innov-is M Series (e.g., M330E): 1 Head (Ultra-compact single-needle home craft style).
  • Innov-is BP Series (e.g., BP3600): 1 Head (Flatbed home luxury single-needle machine).
  • Persona PRS100 / Entrepreneur PR1X: 1 Head (Single-needle tubular free-arm boutique units).
  • PR680W Series: 1 Head (6-needle intermediate semi-commercial model).
  • PR1055X Series: 1 Head (Top-tier 10-needle commercial-grade portable unit).
  • VR Series: 1 Head (Heavy-duty single-needle purely industrial single-head format).

5. Melco (USA)

Melco utilizes an "EMT" modular system where multiple single-heads are linked digitally via software rather than being physically welded onto one structural bridge.

  • Melco Bravo: 1 Head (16-needle desktop commercial format).
  • Melco EMT16X: 1 Head (Modular professional unit. Network software allows linking up to 30 individual physical heads to act as a synced multi-head system).

6. SWF (South Korea)

SWF is distinct for its dual-function machinery, allowing two separate design tasks to execute simultaneously.

  • MAN-12 / MAS-12: 1 Head (Compact single-head multi-needle lines).
  • KX / KS Series: 1 Head (Heavy industrial bridge-style single head).
  • Dual-Function (DF / DM Series): 2 Heads, 4 Heads, 6 Heads, or 8 Heads (The head configurations are split into independent pairs, meaning a 4-head machine can run two heads on a jacket back while the other two run hats on a completely separate pattern).

7. ZSK (Germany)

ZSK focuses heavily on high-precision German manufacturing for standard apparel, smart textiles, and industrial technical fabrics.

  • Sprint Series (Sprint 7 / 8): 1 Head (Premium precision boutique single-head line).
  • Racer Series: 1 Head, 2 Heads, 4 Heads, or 6 Heads (Tubular multi-head system built for intense continuous factory environments).
  • Challenger Series: 10 Heads, 12 Heads, 15 Heads, 18 Heads, or up to 24 Heads (Giant heavy flatbed configurations).
